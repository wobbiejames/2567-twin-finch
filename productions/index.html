brew the coffee

the significance of relying on a high volume of hot air to roast coffee beans can't be understated. most small coffee roasters use a single stage burner which offers virtually no control during the roasting process. the refractory significantly reduces hot spots which can damage coffee beans and produces a much more even roasting environment.
